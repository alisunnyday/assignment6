    About

    Hi! I'm Alison Sun. I'm currently a fourth-year undergraduate at UC Santa Cruz studying computer science and linguistics. As a result of my interdisciplinary exploration of career paths within my studies, I've had the privilege of working on code for a career mentorship conversational UX app, and acting as a research assistant for an ongoing project on the syntax of Sino-Korean eventive nominals.

    Currently, my focus is on spearheading the planning process of a large-scale student outreach event affiliated with UCSC's Baskin School of Engineering, as well as expanding my technical skillset by taking upper-division courses in advanced phonology, machine learning, database systems, and more.

    When I'm not coding or analyzing phonological datasets, you can find me reading Asian-American literary fiction or cafe-hopping in Santa Cruz.
